Tuna Salad

Tuna salad is a great heathy meal that is simple to make

Follow this recipe to make a great meal

Ingredients

  1. Canned Tuna: Any can works, I prefer in brine
  2. Mayonnaise This helps to bind the ingredients
  3. Red Onion: Diced
  4. Parsley: Fresh is best
  5. Celery: Provides the crunch
  6. Lettuce: Extra crunch
  7. Salt and Pepper: to taste

How to make tuna salad

The first step is drain the tuna if your tuna comes in oil do not drain it down the sink

Add the tuna to a bowl, add mayonnaise and stir while breaking up any large tuna chuncks, after this add the remaining ingredients, stir until your preferred consistency.
It's that simple
